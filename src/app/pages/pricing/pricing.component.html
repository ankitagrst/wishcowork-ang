Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and membership options.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your membership plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Monthly plans have no long-term commitment and can be cancelled with 30 days notice. We also offer annual plans with significant discounts for longer commitments.

What's included in meeting room credits?

Meeting room credits allow you to book our conference rooms and private meeting spaces. Each credit represents one hour of meeting room usage, and unused credits roll over monthly.
